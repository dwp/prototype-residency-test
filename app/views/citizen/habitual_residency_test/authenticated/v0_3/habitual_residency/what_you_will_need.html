Before you start

For the Habitual Residency test you will need to do the following:

  • provide copies of the evidence asked for
  • a way to take a photograph, such as a smartphone, tablet or digital camera

Your personal information

We will only use your photographs to check your identity. Once we have done this, we will delete them.
